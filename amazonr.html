Meet the Amazons

The Amazons wore trousers. They smoked pot, covered their skin with tattoos, rode horses, and fought as hard as the men. Legends sprang up like weeds They mutilated or killed their boy children! Modern (mostly male) scholars continued the confabulations. The Amazons were hard-core feminists. Man haters. Delinquent mothers and Lesbians.
